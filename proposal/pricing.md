- 25% on contract execution
- 25% on Phase 1 milestone (architecture draft + Reports audit signed off)

## Assumptions

- Meridian provides timely access to a test environment or confirms local development setup is sufficient
- Meridian stakeholders (Tanaka's team, IT) are available for milestone reviews within 3 business days of delivery
- Scope changes requested after Phase 1 kickoff will be assessed and priced as change orders
- Travel is not required; all work is delivered remotely
